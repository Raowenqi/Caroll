Light

Light plays a crucial role in photography, since photography is essentially about capturing light.

1.Brightness and contrast: The brightness and contrast of a photograph can be adjusted by manipulating the strength and position of the light source.
2.Color: The color and intensity of light also affect the colors of a photograph.
3.Texture and shape: Light can emphasize the texture and shape of an object by shining on it at a specific angle and position.
4.Motion: In capturing sports and other forms of motion, the speed and position of light can affect the blur and sense of motion in the photograph.


Smooth light

we often say that the front light, the light source and the direction of the camera is the same as the smooth light, no matter which direction the person is facing;
this light can be a full expression of the details of the face of the person being photographed, the transition between light and dark is not obvious.

Side light

can be stronger to express the three-dimensional sense of the character's face and lines, while producing a more obvious light and dark transition effect, that is, let the light from the side of the character.

Backlighting

light source from the back of the subject, mainly used to outline the shape of the subject's silhouette.
Also known as silhouette light, because by choosing a different metering point, you can shoot a stronger and pure black shadow of the person, so that you can shoot a more beautiful silhouette light and silhouette effect.
